About

a timeline.

  • 2007-2012

    Georgia Southern University

    Kaleigh recieved her Bachelors degree in 2012 from Georgia Southern University, in Information Technology. She specialized in Web and Multimedia, and also earned a 2nd discipline in Music Technology, as well as a Music minor.

  • 2012-2014

    University of Georgia

    Kaleigh graduated in May 2014 from the University of Georgia, with her Masters in Internet Technology. In the MIT program, Kaleigh gained project management experience, as well as honed her IT skills touching on MVC frameworks, eCommerce, database management, and UX/UI design and development.

  • 2011-Present

    Freelance

    Kaleigh creates new and redesigns existing websites for clients using HTML, CSS, Javascript, Wordpress, or other content management systems. She enjoys setting up and managing social media accounts. Kaleigh is also available for web consulting.

Services

what can I do for you?

Responsive Web Development

I enjoy using the Twitter Bootstrap framework when I create or redesign most websites because it uses HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript to not only create a visually appealing website, but it makes the website responsive and looks beautiful on mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.

WordPress Blogs/Websites

I love using Wordpress to set up blogs and websites. It is easier for my clients to update their information. There are so many Wordpress tools, plugins, themes, and resources which makes it incredibly helpful and fun to use.

Social Media Management

I enjoy helping others create and manage their social media accounts. Sometimes owning so many accounts can seem scary to those who may not know how to utilize social media to help their business or product grow. I can help!
